Smoked Salmon

Description

Delicious smoky salmon will leave your mouth watering

Ingredients

  • Cherry Smoking Chips
  • Salmon Fillets
  • Brown Sugar
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. The day before (dry brine): Sprinkle the salmon fillets with salt, brown sugar, onion powder, and garlic powder. Place it on a platter uncovered in the refrigerator 4 to 6 hours. Then rinse and refrigerate it uncovered overnight.
  2. The day of smoking: Heat an outdoor smoker to 90 degrees C. Place the salmon in the smoker and cook for 45 minutes to 75 minutes, until the internal temperature reaches 60 degrees C.
